Organizing Hierarchies

Boxes in Boxes

  • Elements are boxes.
  • Attributes distinguish box types. For example: <div type="book"> vs. <div type="chapter"> vs. <quote speaker="Bob">

Family Tree

  • Element hierarchies resemble the descent of a family tree.
  • Relationships between elements are referred to similarily. For example:
    1. ancestor
