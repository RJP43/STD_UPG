*Get Involved*

How to become an inducted member:

  1. Students must have earned at least a 3.0 GPA, and have completed the equivalent of three semesters work, or 45 credits.
  2. In addition, students must have taken at least two English Literature or English Writing courses beyond Composition 0020, and must have earned at least a B (3.0) average in those English courses.

How to become an Associated Member (Friend of the Chapter):

  1. No GPA/Credit Hour requirement
  2. Become involved in Chapter events

How to become an Alumni Member:

Sigma Tau Delta members are members for life, and become alumni when they cease current enrollment as student or faculty members in a college or university with an active chapter.
